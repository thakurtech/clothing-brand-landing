Our Heritage

A legacy of excellence spanning over a century.

1895

The Founding

Established in Paris by master tailor Claude Élégance, creating custom garments for European aristocracy.

1925

Art Deco Revolution

Pioneered the integration of Art Deco elements into haute couture, revolutionizing fashion.

1953

Royal Appointment

Received the prestigious Royal Warrant, becoming the official clothier to European royalty.

2023

Modern Legacy

Continuing our tradition of excellence while embracing innovative techniques and sustainable practices.
